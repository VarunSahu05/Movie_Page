Iron Man

Character Name: Tony Stark

Abilities: Genius-level intellect, skilled engineer and inventor, powered armor suit with advanced technology.

Notable Films: Iron Man, The Avengers, Avengers: Endgame.

Tony Stark, a billionaire industrialist, becomes the armored superhero Iron Man after building a high-tech suit to escape captivity. He uses his intellect and resources to protect the world from various threats.

Thor

Character Name: Thor Odinson

Abilities: God-like strength, control over thunder and lightning, skilled warrior, wields the enchanted hammer Mjolnir.

Notable Films: Thor, Thor: Ragnarok, Avengers: Infinity War.

The God of Thunder, Thor, is a powerful warrior from Asgard who wields Mjolnir, his enchanted hammer. He defends both Asgard and Earth from numerous threats, often alongside his fellow Avengers.

Captain America

Character Name: Steve Rogers

Abilities: Enhanced strength, agility, and durability from the Super Soldier Serum, expert tactician and hand-to-hand combatant, iconic shield.

Notable Films: Captain America: The First Avenger, The Avengers, Captain America: Civil War.

Steve Rogers, a scrawny young man, becomes the super-soldier Captain America after being injected with the Super Soldier Serum during World War II. He fights for justice and freedom, leading the Avengers against formidable foes.

Black Widow

Character Name: Natasha Romanoff

Abilities: Expert spy and assassin, master martial artist, skilled marksman.

Notable Films: Iron Man 2, The Avengers, Black Widow.

Natasha Romanoff, known as Black Widow, is a former Russian spy turned Avenger. She uses her combat skills and espionage expertise to fight for justice alongside her fellow heroes.

Captain Marvel

Character Name: Carol Danvers

Abilities: Superhuman strength, flight, energy projection, durability, and regenerative healing.

Notable Films: Captain Marvel, Avengers: Endgame.

Carol Danvers, aka Captain Marvel, is a former Air Force pilot transformed by an alien encounter. She wields immense power and becomes one of the universe's most powerful heroes, fighting for justice and protecting the innocent.

Hulk

Character Name: Bruce Banner

Abilities: Superhuman strength, durability, and regenerative healing.

Notable Films: The Incredible Hulk, The Avengers, Avengers: Age of Ultron.

Dr. Bruce Banner, transformed by gamma radiation, becomes the Hulk, a giant green-skinned monster with incredible strength. Struggling to control his alter ego, Banner seeks redemption and battles villains.

Doctor Strange

Character Name: Stephen Strange

Abilities: Mastery of mystic arts, sorcery, and magical artifacts.

Notable Films: Doctor Strange, Thor: Ragnarok, Avengers: Infinity War.

Brilliant neurosurgeon Stephen Strange seeks healing after a crippling accident. He discovers the mystic arts, becoming Doctor Strange, a powerful sorcerer protecting Earth from magical threats.

Spider-Man

Character Name: Peter Parker

Abilities: Superhuman strength, agility, web-slinging, and precognitive "spider-sense".

Notable Films: Spider-Man: Homecoming, Avengers: Infinity War, Spider-Man: Far From Home.

Teenager Peter Parker gains powers after a radioactive spider bite. As Spider-Man, he balances high school life with fighting crime, mentored by Tony Stark and joining the Avengers.

Thanos

Character Name: Thanos

Abilities: Superhuman strength, durability, and cosmic energy manipulation.

Notable Films: The Avengers, Guardians of the Galaxy, Avengers: Infinity War.

Thanos, a powerful Titan, seeks balance in the universe through destruction and resource control. He collects Infinity Stones to fulfill his twisted vision, clashing with the Avengers.
